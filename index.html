Zheyang Xiong

PhD Student, Computer Science, University of Wisconsin-Madison

Advisor: Dimitris Papailiopoulos

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, advised by Dimitris Papailiopoulos. I received my B.S. in Mathematics and B.A. in Computer Science from Rice University in 2023.

My research focuses on understanding and improving large language models (LLMs), with particular interests in in-context learning, reasoning, and retrieval capabilities of LLMs.

I am also a part-time student researcher at Microsoft Research AI Frontiers Lab.
